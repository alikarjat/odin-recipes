Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, and cheese.

Some variations include vegetables or different types of cheese, but the classic
version typically includes ground beef, tomato sauce, and ricotta cheese.

Prepare this easy homemade lasagne ahead of time and save in the freezer, unc-
ooked, for when you need it during a busy week. Just bake for an extra 45 mins

Ingredients

  • Lasagne noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagne noodles according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned.
  4. Add the tomato sauce to the skillet with the ground beef and simmer for 10
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  7. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Let the lasagne cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
