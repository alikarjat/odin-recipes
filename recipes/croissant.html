Croissant

Description

A flaky, buttery pastry that is a staple in French bakeries and cafes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup butter, cold and cubed
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup warm water

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. Add cold butter and work it into the flour mixture until it resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Add warm water gradually and mix until a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough gently on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Roll out the dough into a large rectangle and fold it three times like a letter.
  6. Roll out again and cut into triangles. Roll each triangle from the wide end to the tip to form croissants.
  7. Place croissants on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake in a preheated oven at 400°F (200°C) for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.
